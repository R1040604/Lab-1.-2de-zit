In Focus: Our Start Full of Light and Inspiration

We are kicking off our photography club in early October with a creative blast: the FlashWalk. During this group photo walk, we will explore the city together at sunset, searching for the interplay of light, color, and shadow. It will be an accessible and inspiring way to get to know each other and immediately take the first photos for our community. The FlashWalk will be followed by an introductory workshop, where you will receive practical tips on composition, lighting, and creative storytelling with your camera or smartphone. This workshop is perfect for both beginners and experienced photographers who want to gain new inspiration. At the same time, we will launch our social media campaign to promote our new club and get future members excited. This will culminate in our first major highlight: the official opening of our first exhibition. During this exhibition, we will showcase the work of our members and celebrate the launch of a club that connects, inspires, and makes photography accessible to everyone.

What can you expect?

A fun and creative evening walk where you will get to know the city in a new way and learn how to photograph it. We will focus on capturing the interplay between light and shadow at sunset, with plenty of opportunity to ask questions and exchange tips. Afterwards, there will be a practical workshop where you can further develop your photography skills.

What are you bringing?

Camera or smartphone, whatever you have and use. Fully charged battery and enough storage space (or an empty memory card). Possibly a tripod, especially useful in low light conditions. Comfortable shoes and weatherproof clothing. A small flashlight or headlamp for dark areas. A desire to learn and be creative together!

Why participate?

Join us to share your passion for photography, meet new people, and find inspiration in a welcoming, friendly atmosphere. Whether you're a beginner or already have experience, you'll discover new perspectives, improve your technique, and become part of a creative community that motivates and challenges you. Together, we'll make photography a connecting and inspiring experience!
