Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need an expensive camera to participate?

No, definitely not! You can participate with any camera, including your smartphone. It's all about creativity and fun, not equipment.

Is participation free?

Yes. Becoming a member of our photography club and participating in the FlashWalk is free of charge. You only pay for any personal expenses, such as food or drinks along the way.

No. Everyone is welcome: from absolute beginner to experienced photographer. Our activities are designed so that you always learn something new.

How can I register for the FlashWalk?

Through the registration form on our website. After signing up, you will receive a confirmation with all the practical information.

How long does the FlashWalk last?

On average, 1.5 to 2 hours, including stops to take photos and exchange tips.

Does the FlashWalk go ahead in bad weather?

We love to photograph in all conditions, but in case of heavy rain or storms, we will move the activity to a new date. You will be notified in advance.

Can I also participate in the workshop without the FlashWalk?

Yes, you can! The workshop is separate from the FlashWalk and is accessible to anyone interested in photography.

What other activities does the club organize?

In addition to the FlashWalk, we organize photo walks, workshops, challenges, exhibitions, and even collaborations with other art forms such as music, dance, and poetry.

Can I share my photos from the FlashWalk on social media?

Yes, you can! We encourage participants to share their photos and tag us on social media.

How can I stay updated on new activities?

You can sign up for our newsletter via the website. Additionally, you can follow us on social media for the latest updates.

How can I stay updated on new activities?

By becoming a member through our website. You will then receive our newsletter and gain access to our private online community.
